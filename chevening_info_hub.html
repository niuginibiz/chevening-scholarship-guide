Chevening Scholarship Opportunity

A guide for DJAG ICT Staff for the 2026-2027 intake.

Unlock Your Potential with a UK Master's Degree

The Chevening Scholarship is a prestigious, fully funded program by the UK Government, enabling outstanding emerging leaders from all over the world to pursue a one-year masterâ€™s degree in the UK. This is a fantastic opportunity for professional development and to become part of a global network of leaders. This page simplifies the application process for you.

What you get

A fully funded scholarship covering university tuition fees, monthly living allowance, travel costs to and from the UK, and more.

Study Period

One academic year for a Master's Degree programme, starting in September/October 2026.

Key Deadline

Online applications typically close in early November. You must also submit your internal DJAG bid concurrently.

Are You Eligible?

Before you begin, check if you meet the main requirements for the Chevening Scholarship. You must:

1

Be a citizen of PNG

And intend to return for a minimum of two years after your award has ended.

2

Have an Undergraduate Degree

This is required to be accepted into a UK master's programme.

3

Have Work Experience

At least two years of work experience (equivalent to 2,800 hours).

4

Not have prior UK study

You must not have previously studied in the UK with funding from a UK Government-funded scholarship.

The Application Process: A Step-by-Step Guide

Applying involves two parallel processes: the internal DJAG nomination bid and the external Chevening online application. You must complete both.

Step 1: Research & Prepare

Explore the Chevening website and identify three eligible one-year master's courses at UK universities. Prepare your personal essays and gather your required documents early.

1

Step 2: Submit DJAG Training Bid

Complete the "Public Service Training Bid Form" and submit it with all required documents to the DJAG Human Resource Management Branch for internal processing and endorsement.

2

Step 3: Apply Online to Chevening

Concurrently, complete and submit your application directly on the official Chevening website before their global deadline.
